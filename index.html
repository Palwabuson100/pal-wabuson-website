Introduction

Africa's future will be shaped by the people willing to build it.

Across the continent, ambitious businesses are trying to grow with limited resources, rising operational costs and systems that were not designed for their realities.

I believe artificial intelligence can help change that. Not as a distant idea, but as a practical tool that helps businesses work faster, serve customers better and grow with greater efficiency.

That belief is what led me to Unovia Technologies.

Timeline

The path has not been linear.

Music, real estate and technology each became a different classroom for building.

01

Pal Bass Academy

My entrepreneurial journey began at university.

Pal Bass Academy was a music education platform that reached students in more than 35 countries. It started with a passion for music but became my first experience of building a product, teaching a global audience and growing a digital community.

02

Real Estate

More than three years as a top-selling realtor.

I built and led a sales team that generated billions of naira in sales. Real estate taught me how to sell, lead, negotiate and understand the decisions people make around money, security and opportunity.

03

Technology

My transition into technology came from seeing operational pressure up close.

Missed enquiries. Repetitive tasks. Expensive processes. Small teams carrying too much responsibility. I became interested in how artificial intelligence could solve these problems in a practical way. That curiosity became Unovia Technologies.

Lions Path Initiative

Growth should not happen in isolation.

I am the convener of Lions Path Initiative, a private community of men committed to accountability, support and personal development.

The community exists to create space for honest conversations, meaningful relationships and shared growth. It is built around the belief that men become stronger when they have the right people around them.
