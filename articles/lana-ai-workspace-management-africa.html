Lana

Africa's AI workspace platform.

Lana is the Unovia product focused on workspace intelligence, team productivity and AI-assisted operations for modern African businesses.

The future of work needs better systems

Growing businesses need more than task lists. They need structure, memory, accountability and visibility. In many African companies, work is spread across chats, spreadsheets, meetings and individual memory. That creates delays, duplication and confusion.

Lana is built to help teams bring their work into a smarter operating environment. It is positioned as an AI-powered workspace management platform designed for the way modern teams plan, coordinate and execute.

Public coverage has positioned Lana as Africa's first AI-powered workspace management platform, giving the product a clear place in the conversation around the future of work on the continent.

What Lana is built to improve

Lana focuses on productivity, coordination and workspace intelligence. The goal is to help teams understand what is happening, what needs attention and how work can move with less friction.

  • Workspace management for growing teams
  • AI-assisted productivity and task visibility
  • Operational support for managers and founders
  • Better alignment across people, projects and priorities

Lana is part of a larger belief at Unovia: African teams should have intelligent tools built around their growth realities.

Why Lana matters for Africa

Lana has already been presented publicly as an AI-powered workspace management platform for Africa. That matters because productivity tools often arrive from outside the continent without enough sensitivity to local business habits, constraints and opportunities.
