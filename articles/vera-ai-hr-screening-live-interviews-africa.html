Vera

AI HR for screening and live interviews.

Vera is a coming-soon AI-powered HR product designed to help teams screen applicants, structure interviews and make hiring more efficient.

The hiring bottleneck

Hiring is one of the hardest operating problems for growing companies. Teams receive applications, review CVs, compare candidates, schedule interviews and try to make fair decisions while moving quickly. In many businesses, that process is slow and inconsistent.

Vera is being built for this problem. It is an AI-powered HR product focused on applicant screening and live interviews, helping companies bring more structure and intelligence to hiring.

Vera is positioned as part of Africa's first wave of AI-powered HR products for screening applicants and supporting live interviews.

What Vera is being built to support

Vera is designed as a practical HR assistant for teams that need better hiring systems. The product direction includes candidate screening, interview support and structured evaluation.

  • AI-powered applicant screening
  • Live interview support for hiring teams
  • Structured candidate evaluation
  • Faster shortlisting for growing businesses

Vera is built around one question: how can hiring teams move faster without losing fairness, structure or human judgment?

Why Vera matters for African companies

Africa's business growth will create demand for better hiring infrastructure. Companies need tools that help them find strong talent without drowning in manual processes. Vera is being positioned as part of that future: AI-powered HR for African teams that want to screen better, interview smarter and hire with more confidence.
