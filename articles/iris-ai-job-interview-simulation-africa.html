Iris

AI interview simulation for African talent.

Iris is an AI-powered job interview simulation platform being built to help job seekers practice, improve and walk into interviews with more confidence.

The interview preparation gap

Many talented people lose opportunities not because they lack ability, but because they have not had enough realistic interview practice. They may not know how to structure answers, handle pressure, explain their experience or prepare for role-specific questions.

Iris is being built to make interview preparation more accessible. It gives talent a way to practice with AI, receive structure and build confidence before the real conversation.

It is positioned as an Africa-first AI interview simulation platform for the next generation of job seekers, graduates and professionals.

What Iris is designed to do

Iris focuses on job interview simulation. The product direction is to help users rehearse interviews, understand the kinds of questions they may face and improve the clarity of their answers.

  • AI-powered interview practice
  • Role-aware question simulation
  • Confidence-building for job seekers
  • Preparation support for students, graduates and professionals

Iris is built around a human truth: access to practice can change how people show up for opportunity.

Why Iris matters in Africa

Africa has a young, ambitious and fast-growing workforce. But access to quality career preparation is uneven. Iris is being positioned as an AI-powered interview simulation platform for African talent, helping people prepare for better jobs and stronger career transitions.
